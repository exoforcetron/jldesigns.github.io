Project SEKAI COLORFUL STAGE! feat. Hatsune Miku is a mobile rhythem game created by a collab between SEGA, Craft Egg, Colorful Palette, and Crypton Future Media for android and IOS.

Story

The game is set in Shibuya, Tokyo. A girl named Hoshino Ichika, walks through the streets and sees an LCD screen displaying Hatsune Miku. Upon returning home, she discovers a song named "Untitled" on her smartphone. Out of curiously, Ichika plays the song, only to get surrounded by a white light, and arrives at an unknown place. Miku stands in front of Ichika, welcoming her to Sekai, and inviting her to sing together.
Sekai is a mysterious world that reflects people's "real feelings" that is different from the real world. There are as many sekai as there are feelings, and their appearance changes according to the feelings.
